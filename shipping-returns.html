Shipping & Returns

Dispatch & Delivery Timeline

Order payment confirm hone ke baad dispatch typically 24-48 hours me hota hai. Ahmedabad city deliveries usually 1-2 business days me, Gujarat within 2-4 business days, aur interstate 4-7 business days me complete hoti hain.

Return Period

Return request delivery ke 3 days ke andar raise karna hoga. Product unused, damage-free aur original packing me hona chahiye.

Replacement / Exchange Timeline

Approved replacement/exchange request ke baad replacement product 3-7 business days ke andar dispatch kar diya jata hai (location par depend karega).
