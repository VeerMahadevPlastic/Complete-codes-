Shipping & Returns

Shipping charges order quantity, parcel size (volumetric), and delivery zone par depend karte hain. Final charge checkout ya order confirmation par confirm hota hai.

Dispatch timeline: paid orders usually 24-48 hours me dispatch hote hain (stock availability ke basis par).

Damaged / wrong item case me 24 hours ke andar WhatsApp support par photos ke saath report karein.

Custom/used/opened products return eligible nahi hote. Bulk orders me replacement policy applicable ho sakti hai.
