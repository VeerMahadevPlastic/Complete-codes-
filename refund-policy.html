Shipping & Refund Policy

Shipping: All orders from Veer Mahadev Plastic are dispatched from our Ahmedabad warehouse within 24-48 hours of order confirmation. Delivery usually takes 2-5 business days depending on the location.

Returns

Being a wholesale manufacturer, we only accept returns if the product is damaged during transit or if the wrong item was delivered.

Refund Process

To claim a refund or replacement, the customer must provide an unboxing video within 24 hours of delivery. Once verified, the refund will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 working days.

Cancellation

Orders can only be cancelled before they are dispatched. Once shipped, cancellations are not permitted.
