The Veer Mahadev Legacy

Founded in 2022 as a family-driven enterprise, Veer Mahadev Plastic has rapidly grown into a cornerstone of the disposable industry. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality food packaging at direct factory prices.

Today, we take pride in serving more than 10,000 monthly customers, ranging from local street vendors to premium cafes and large-scale event planners. Our commitment remains rooted in hygiene, durability, and a sustainable shift toward biodegradable alternatives.
