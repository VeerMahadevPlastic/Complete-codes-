Privacy Policy

Veer Mahadev Plastic (Ahmedabad, Gujarat) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and place retail or wholesale orders (including our 5,000-unit wholesale MOQ programs).

2. Information We Collect

  • Name

We may also collect non-personal data such as browser type, IP address, and usage analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data. We share only required data with trusted partners (payment gateways, logistics and delivery providers) for order fulfillment. We may disclose information when legally required by authorities.

5. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect personal data. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to improve website usability and performance. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.
