Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Aapki privacy humare liye important hai. Yeh Privacy Policy batati hai ki hum aapki personal information kaise collect, use aur protect karte hain jab aap hamari website use karte ho.

2. Information We Collect

Hum aapse kuch personal details collect kar sakte hain, jaise:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping/Billing address
  • Payment details (secure gateway ke through)

Saath hi, hum non-personal data bhi collect karte hain jaise browser type, IP address, aur website usage data.

3. How We Use Your Information

Aapki information ka use hum karte hain:

  • Orders process karne ke liye
  • Delivery manage karne ke liye
  • Customer support provide karne ke liye
  • Offers, updates aur promotions bhejne ke liye (agar aap allow karein)
  • Website improve karne ke liye

4. Sharing of Information

Hum aapki personal information kisi third party ko sell nahi karte.

Kuch trusted partners (payment gateways, delivery services) ke saath share karte hain sirf order complete karne ke liye.

Legal requirement hone par authorities ke saath share kar sakte hain.

5. Data Security

Hum reasonable security measures use karte hain aapki information protect karne ke liye.

Lekin internet par 100% security guarantee nahi hoti.

6. Cookies

Website cookies use karti hai better user experience ke liye.

Aap browser settings se cookies disable kar sakte hain.
