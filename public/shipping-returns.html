Shipping & Returns

Dispatch & Delivery

Orders are dispatched after payment confirmation. Typical dispatch time is 24-48 hours from our Ahmedabad manufacturing/warehouse base. Estimated delivery: Ahmedabad 1-2 business days, Gujarat 2-4 business days, interstate 4-7 business days.

Return Window

Return requests must be raised within 3 days from delivery. Items should be unused, hygienic, and in original packaging.

Replacement / Exchange Timeline

Approved replacement or exchange shipments are usually dispatched within 3-7 business days depending on stock and route availability.

Wholesale Note

Wholesale orders may be governed by MOQ commitments (typically 5,000 units+) and dispatch plans agreed at the time of confirmation.
